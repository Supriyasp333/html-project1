Gulab jamun
Gulab jamun are a milk-solid-based sweet from the Indian subcontinent, originating in northern India.

Modak
Modak is an Indian sweet popular in many parts of India.

Gajar ka halwa
Gajar ka halwa is a carrot-based sweet dessert pudding from the Indian subcontinent.

Ladoo
Laddu or laddoo are sphere-shaped sweets originated in the Indian subcontinent and Nepal.

Cham Cham
Chomchom, cham cham, or chum chum (Bengali: চমচম) is a traditional Bengali sweet originated from Porabari, Tangail, Bangladesh.

Ras malai
The name ras malai is the Hindi cognate which comes from two words in Bengali: rosh, meaning "juice", and molai, meaning "cream".

Rasgulla
Rasgulla is a syrupy dessert popular in the Indian subcontinent and regions with South Asian diaspora.

Jalebi
Jalebi, also known as zulbia and zalabia, is a sweet popular food in some parts of South Asia, West Asia, North Africa, and East Africa.
